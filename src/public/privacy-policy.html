Privacy Policy

Stay Woke, Campaign Zero and affiliated organizations will not share this information with any other entity without your express permission. We will also not comply with a government subpoena for any information that is shared. We will require a warrant authorized by a judge for any information requested and will notify you of that request unless expressly ordered not to by a judge.

Unfortunately, we cannot provide assurances that the host of this database, Amazon, will not comply with a government subpoena or notify us if presented with a subpoena. Additionally, any text messages are handled by Twilio, and we cannot provide any assurances that they will not comply with a government subpoena or notify us if presented with a subpoena.
